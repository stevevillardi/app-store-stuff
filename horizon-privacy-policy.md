# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 27, 2026

## Overview

Horizon ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information Collection and Use

Horizon accesses your device's calendar data solely to display, classify, and organize your events within the app. All calendar data is processed entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your calendar data on any external servers.

## On-Device Processing

All event classification and intelligence features run on-device using Apple's Core ML framework. No calendar event content, titles, attendee information, or scheduling details ever leave your device.

## Data Stored on Device

Horizon stores a local cache of your calendar events using Apple's SwiftData framework to provide a responsive experience. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## CloudKit Sync

Horizon may use Apple's CloudKit to sync your preferences and custom category settings across your devices. Only app preferences are synced — never calendar event content, personal data, or authentication tokens. This sync is handled entirely through Apple's infrastructure and is subject to Apple's privacy policies.

## Third-Party Services

Horizon does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app connects only to your existing calendar providers (such as iCloud, Google Calendar, or Microsoft Outlook) using your own credentials, which are managed by the operating system and never stored by Horizon.

## Children's Privacy

Horizon does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not include any functionality for collecting personally identifiable information.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. You will be notified of changes by the updated date at the top of this policy.
